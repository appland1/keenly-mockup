Help & Tips
What is a social story?
Short descriptive stories that help children understand social situations, expectations, and emotions.
How do I create one?
Tap the + tile on the home screen. Add photos from your library, type captions, and record audio if you want narration.
Can I share stories?
Yes — long-press any story tile and pick "Share Story" from the menu.
How do I rearrange pages?
Open a story, tap the menu icon, choose "Arrange." Drag pages with the grip handle.
